Why no broker logins?

This app will never ask for a brokerage username or password. "Encrypted in the code" is a myth — if the app can decrypt it, so can anyone who gets the file. Everything automatic here (prices, dividends, calendars, TV fill imports) works from read-only data you export or a quotes-only API key. Your money stays behind your broker's own login, where it belongs.
